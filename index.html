About Us:

Ignited Minds is a Social Enterprise Started in January 2019.Ignited Minds engaged in STEM education and WASH sector. Ignited Minds major work comprises of establishing STEM educational platforms ,WASH platforms at School and community level. Ignited Minds also working for Capacity building of Teachers, Students and related stakeholders through its innovative methods. Ignited Minds work on research and development in STEM education and WASH sector.

Our Vision:

We will work to improve educational outcomes by establishing STEM education, WASH platforms and creating infrastructure for complementary education such as Science Park, STEM labs, Science Labs, play grounds, WASH clubs and Skill development Programs at schools.
